# Back in Shape
**2 weeks** | General focus

A 2-week general physical preparedness program designed to rebuild foundational strength, movement quality, and work capacity. Progressive loading from 70-80% 1RM across the cycle with balanced push/pull volume and varied energy system development.

---

## W1D1 — Lower Body Strength + Short Metcon
**Duration:** 60 min | **Week 1, Day 1**

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- rack
- pull-up bar
- kettlebell

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Hip Circles — 10 each direction
- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Focus on thoracic extension*
- PVC Pass-Through — 15 reps — *Wide grip*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 5 each side

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Air Squat — 15 reps — *Pause 2 sec in bottom*
- Good Morning (PVC or empty bar) — 10 reps — *Hinge pattern prep*
- Inchworm — 8 reps
- Banded Pull-Apart — 20 reps — *Shoulder activation*

### 💪 Strength — Back Squat (Straight Sets) (22 min)

- **Back Squat** — 5×5 @ 70% 1RM | Rest 180s
  > *Week 1 baseline. Focus on depth and midline stability.*
  > **Scale:** Goblet squat 4x8 if 1RM unknown or returning from layoff

### 🔥 Metcon — 12 min AMRAP (15 min)

**Format:** AMRAP | **Time cap:** 12 min

- 15 reps Kettlebell Swing (Russian) @ 24/16 kg *(Scale: Reduce weight or reps to 10)*
- 10 reps Pull-Up *(Scale: Banded pull-up or ring row)*
- 5 reps Burpee *(Scale: Step-down burpee)*

**Target:** 5+ rounds

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Foam Roll — Quads — 90 sec each leg
- Pigeon Pose — 90 sec each side — *Hip opener*
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side — *Supine position*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session design:** Week 1 Day 1 establishes baseline squat volume at moderate intensity (70% 1RM). Short-duration metcon (12 min AMRAP) targets glycolytic system without excessive systemic fatigue. Squat + hinge pattern metcon maintains movement synergy while avoiding posterior chain overload. *— Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed*

**Movement selection:** Back squat chosen as primary lower body strength movement for Week 1 baseline. Metcon pairs hinge (KB swing), vertical pull (pull-up), and full-body conditioning (burpee) to balance squat-dominant strength work with posterior chain and upper body pulling volume. *— Haff & Triplett - NSCA Essentials 4th ed*

**Loading rationale:** Straight sets at 70% 1RM for 5x5 structure builds baseline strength and work capacity. This intensity allows technical proficiency while accumulating sufficient volume for structural adaptation. 180-second rest ensures phosphocreatine recovery between sets. *— Zatsiorsky & Kraemer - Science and Practice of Strength Training*

- box

- Hang Power Clean (empty bar) — 8 reps — *Movement prep*
- Front Squat (empty bar) — 8 reps — *Receiving position prep*
- Inchworm — 6 reps

### 💪 Strength — Hang Power Clean (EMOM) (24 min)

- **Hang Power Clean** — 8×3 @ 65% 1RM
  > *E2MOM x 8 rounds (16 min total). Complete 3 reps at top of each 2-min interval. Focus on hip extension and elbow turnover speed.*
  > **Scale:** Dumbbell hang clean or reduce to 60% 1RM if technique breaks down

### 🔥 Metcon — For Time (14 min)

**Format:** For Time | **Time cap:** 12 min

- 500m Row (Ergometer) *(Scale: Reduce to 400m)*
- 20 reps Box Jump @ 24/20 inch box *(Scale: Step-up or reduce box height)*
- 500m Row (Ergometer) *(Scale: Reduce to 400m)*

**Target:** Sub 8 minutes

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (7 min)

- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 sec each side
- Hip Flexor Stretch (90/90) — 90 sec each side
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session design:** Olympic lifting on Day 2 after 24h recovery from squats. EMOM format maintains session flow and auto-regulates rest. Medium metcon (8-12 min target) develops glycolytic capacity with monostructural rowing and power output (box jumps). *— Haff & Triplett - NSCA Essentials 4th ed*

**Movement selection:** Hang power clean targets explosive hip extension and receiving position without full squat demand, allowing recovery from Day 1 squats. Metcon pairs monostructural aerobic work (row) with lower body power (box jump) to develop mixed energy system capacity. *— Gastin 2001 - Energy System Interaction*

**Loading rationale:** EMOM strength format at 65% 1RM for 3 reps exploits auto-regulated rest while building technical proficiency. E2MOM interval provides adequate phosphocreatine recovery for power movements. Lower intensity than squat work allows CNS recovery while maintaining training stimulus. *— Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed*

## W1D4 — Upper Body Push/Pull + Short Metcon
**Duration:** 60 min | **Week 1, Day 4**

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- bench
- pull-up bar
- jump rope

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Shoulder CARs (Controlled Articular Rotations) — 5 each direction
- Cat-Cow — 10 reps
- Banded Pull-Apart — 20 reps — *Scapular activation*
- PVC Pass-Through — 15 reps

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Push-Up — 10 reps — *Strict, full range*
- Ring Row — 10 reps — *Body angle 45 degrees*
- Inchworm — 8 reps
- Jump Rope (single-under) — 50 reps — *Warmup for metcon*

### 💪 Strength — Bench Press (Ramp/Build-Up) (24 min)

- **Bench Press** — 4×5-4-3-2 @ 65%, 70%, 75%, 78% 1RM | Rest 180s
  > *Ramp/build-up sets. Set 1: 5 reps @ 65%, Set 2: 4 reps @ 70%, Set 3: 3 reps @ 75%, Set 4: 2 reps @ 78%. Progressive neural preparation for horizontal push pattern.*
  > **Scale:** Dumbbell bench press with same progression structure

### 🔥 Metcon — 10 min AMRAP (14 min)

**Format:** AMRAP | **Time cap:** 10 min

- 8 reps Chest-to-Bar Pull-Up *(Scale: Regular pull-up or banded pull-up)*
- 12 reps Push-Up *(Scale: Knee push-up or reduce reps to 8)*
- 30 reps Double-Under *(Scale: 90 single-unders (3x substitution))*

**Target:** 4+ rounds

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (7 min)

- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 sec each side
- Pigeon Pose — 60 sec each side
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session design:** Upper body focus after rest day allows lower body recovery. Short metcon (10 min) maintains glycolytic stimulus without excessive fatigue accumulation. Push/pull pairing in both strength and metcon ensures balanced horizontal and vertical plane development. *— Haff & Triplett - NSCA Essentials 4th ed*

**Movement selection:** Bench press (horizontal push) paired with chest-to-bar pull-up metcon (vertical pull) creates balanced upper body volume. Double-unders add monostructural cardio component while maintaining upper body engagement through coordination demand. *— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal*

**Loading rationale:** Ramp/build-up progression (5@65%, 4@70%, 3@75%, 2@78%) provides progressive neural preparation while developing technique under increasing load. This scheme appropriate for Week 1 baseline establishment and allows athletes to find working weights safely. *— Zatsiorsky & Kraemer - Science and Practice of Strength Training*

### 💪 Gymnastics Skill — Pull-Up Progression + Body Tension (22 min)

- **Hollow Body Hold** — 4×20-30 sec | Rest 60s
  > *Foundational body tension work. Maintain lower back contact with floor.*
  > **Scale:** Bent knee hollow hold
- **Arch Body Hold** — 4×20-30 sec | Rest 60s
  > *Complement to hollow position. Focus on glute engagement.*
  > **Scale:** Reduce duration to 15 sec
- **Pull-Up (Strict)** — 5×3-5 | Rest 90s
  > *Quality focus. Full range of motion, controlled descent.*
  > **Scale:** Banded pull-up or jumping pull-up with slow 3-sec negative

### 🔥 Metcon — For Time (16 min)

**Format:** For Time | **Time cap:** 15 min

- 2000m Row (Ergometer) *(Scale: Reduce to 1500m)*

**Target:** Sub 8:30

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (7 min)

- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 sec each side
- Pigeon Pose — 90 sec each side
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session design:** Day 6 gymnastics skill session develops pulling strength and body tension without high CNS demand. Pure aerobic monostructural work (2k row) satisfies weekly aerobic minimum and provides active recovery before rest day. Low interference with strength adaptations. *— Wilson et al 2012 - Concurrent Training Meta-Analysis*

**Movement selection:** Hollow and arch body holds are prerequisite foundational tension for all gymnastics movements. Strict pull-up development builds pulling strength capacity required before kipping progression. Rowing provides low-impact aerobic stimulus. *— Sommer - Building the Gymnastic Body*

**Loading rationale:** Skill work uses moderate volume (4-5 sets) with full rest to prioritize movement quality over fatigue accumulation. Hold durations (20-30 sec) build structural endurance. Pull-up prescription focuses on quality reps (3-5) to develop strict strength foundation. *— CrossFit Gymnastics Specialty Course*
