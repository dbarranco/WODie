# WOD Pool

## WOD-001 — Power Development: Clean Complex + Chipper
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- pull-up bar
- box

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Hip Circles — 10 each direction — *Warming hip joint through full range of motion*
- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm — *Controlled articular rotations for shoulder mobility*
- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Spinal mobility drill*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 3 each side — *Multi-planar hip and thoracic mobility*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Air Squat — 15 reps — *Squat pattern activation*
- Good Morning (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Hinge pattern primer*
- Hang Power Clean (empty bar) — 8 reps — *Movement-specific preparation for power clean*
- Inchworm — 5 reps — *Dynamic hamstring and shoulder warmup*

### 💪 Power Clean Complex — Ramp to Heavy (22 min)

- **Power Clean** — 5×3-2-2-1-1 @ Ramp from 65% to 85% 1RM
  > *Progressively increase load: Set 1 @ 65% x3, Set 2 @ 72% x2, Set 3 @ 78% x2, Set 4 @ 83% x1, Set 5 @ 85% x1. Focus on explosive hip extension and fast elbows.*
  > **Scale:** Hang power clean or dumbbell power clean

### 🔥 Metcon — 21-15-9 reps (15 min)

**Format:** For Time | **Time cap:** 12 min

- 21-15-9 Power Clean @ 60% 1RM or moderate load *(Scale: Reduce load to 50% 1RM)*
- 21-15-9 Pull-Up @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Banded pull-up or ring row)*
- 21-15-9 Box Jump @ 24"/20" box *(Scale: Reduce height or step-ups)*

**Target:** Complete all reps in under 10 minutes

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Foam Roll — Quads — 90 seconds each leg — *Soft tissue release after high-volume squatting pattern*
- Foam Roll — Lats — 60 seconds each side — *Release tension from pulling work*
- Hip Flexor Stretch — 90 seconds each side — *Static stretch appropriate post-workout*
- Pigeon Pose — 90 seconds each side — *Hip external rotation and glute stretch*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session targets power development through Olympic lifting followed by a mixed-modal metcon. The power clean builds rate of force development (Zatsiorsky & Kraemer), while the metcon applies that power output under fatigue. The 21-15-9 format provides descending volume that allows maintenance of intensity as glycogen depletes. Time domain of 8-12 minutes targets glycolytic system without excessive oxidative demand that would interfere with strength adaptation.
*— Zatsiorsky & Kraemer - Science and Practice of Strength Training, Wilson et al 2012 - Concurrent Training Meta-Analysis*

**Movement Selection**  
Power clean is the primary strength movement, training the full kinetic chain from hinge through pull to receiving position. Pull-ups and box jumps in the metcon maintain the power emphasis — pull-ups develop vertical pulling strength (CrossFit's 10 physical skills), and box jumps train lower body power output. This combination addresses strength, power, and coordination across multiple planes.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal (10 Physical Skills), Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed*

**Loading Rationale**  
Ramp/build-up loading scheme is used for Week 1 Olympic lifting to prepare the nervous system progressively for heavier loads while refining technique. Starting at 65% allows technical focus; ending at 85% provides neural stimulus without excessive CNS fatigue. Metcon load is reduced to 60% 1RM to allow barbell cycling while maintaining movement quality under fatigue. Rest periods of 2-3 minutes allow phosphocreatine recovery between power clean sets.
*— Zatsiorsky & Kraemer - Science and Practice of Strength Training Ch.3, Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials Ch.3*

## WOD-002 — Squat Volume + Aerobic Power AMRAP
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- rack
- assault bike
- kettlebell

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Spinal segmentation and mobility*
- Hip Circles — 10 each direction — *Hip joint warmup*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 3 each side — *Hip mobility and thoracic rotation*
- Pigeon Pose — 60 seconds each side — *Hip external rotation stretch*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Air Squat — 20 reps — *Squat pattern activation*
- Goblet Squat — 10 reps (light KB) — *Front-loaded squat primer*
- Inchworm — 5 reps — *Dynamic posterior chain warmup*
- Assault Bike — 2 minutes easy pace — *Elevate heart rate, prepare for cardio work*

### 💪 Front Squat — E2MOM Volume (24 min)

- **Front Squat** — 8×4 @ 75% 1RM (or RPE 7-8)
  > *Perform 4 reps at the top of each 2-minute window. EMOM format auto-regulates rest and maintains session flow. Focus on upright torso and full depth.*
  > **Scale:** Goblet squat or reduce load to 65-70%

### 🔥 Metcon — 15 minutes (15 min)

**Format:** AMRAP | **Time cap:** 15 min

- 12 calories Assault Bike @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Reduce to 9 calories or substitute row)*
- 15 Kettlebell Swing (Russian) @ 53/35 lbs *(Scale: Reduce reps or use lighter kettlebell)*
- 10 Burpee @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Step-down burpee or reduce reps)*

**Target:** Target 6-8 rounds. Steady pace, not maximal effort.

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Assault Bike (easy pace) — 3 minutes — *Active recovery to flush metabolites*
- Foam Roll — Quads — 90 seconds each leg — *Release tension from squat volume*
- Hip Flexor Stretch — 90 seconds each side — *Static hip flexor stretch post-workout*
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side — *Restore thoracic mobility after front rack position*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session prioritizes lower body structural strength through front squat volume followed by a longer aerobic AMRAP. The 15-minute time domain places primary demand on the oxidative system (Gastin 2001), developing aerobic capacity while minimizing interference with the strength work. The AMRAP format teaches pacing and work capacity management. This session fits the weekly requirement for at least one aerobic-emphasis piece.
*— Gastin 2001 - Energy System Interaction, Wilson et al 2012 - Concurrent Training Meta-Analysis*

**Movement Selection**  
Front squat develops squat pattern strength with anterior loading, improving thoracic extension and core stability. The metcon combines monostructural cardio (assault bike), hinge pattern (KB swing), and full-body movement (burpee) to distribute load across multiple muscle groups, preventing localized fatigue. This variety sustains movement quality over 15 minutes and addresses CrossFit's broad modal training principle.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal, Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials 4th ed*

**Loading Rationale**  
E2MOM strength format at 75% 1RM (Week 2 intensity) allows consistent volume accumulation without excessive rest periods. The 2-minute interval provides adequate phosphocreatine recovery for 4-rep sets at this intensity while maintaining session flow. EMOM format rewards efficient execution and prevents sandbagging. Metcon movements are intentionally moderate intensity to preserve aerobic pacing rather than glycolytic sprint efforts.
*— Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed, Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials Ch.3*

## WOD-003 — Deadlift Strength + Sprint Couplet
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- pull-up bar

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Spinal mobility warmup*
- Hip Circles — 10 each direction — *Hip joint preparation*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 3 each side — *Multi-planar hip and thoracic mobility*
- Foam Roll — Lats — 60 seconds each side — *Pre-mobilize lats for deadlift setup*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Good Morning (empty bar) — 12 reps — *Hinge pattern activation*
- Deadlift (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Movement-specific warmup for deadlift*
- Glute Bridge — 15 reps — *Glute activation before heavy pulling*
- Pull-Up — 5 reps — *Prime pulling pattern for metcon*

### 💪 Deadlift — Wave Loading (22 min)

- **Deadlift** — 6×3-3-3-3-3-3 @ Wave: 75%-80%-85%-75%-82%-87% 1RM
  > *Wave loading exploits post-activation potentiation. After heavy set at 85%, the 75% set will feel lighter due to neural priming. Maintain neutral spine throughout.*
  > **Scale:** Reduce intensity by 5-10% or use trap bar deadlift

### 🔥 Metcon — 5 Rounds (12 min)

**Format:** For Time | **Time cap:** 10 min

- 10 Deadlift @ 60% 1RM or moderate load *(Scale: Reduce load to 50% or reduce reps to 7)*
- 10 Chest-to-Bar Pull-Up @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Regular pull-up or banded pull-up)*

**Target:** Complete 5 rounds in 7-9 minutes. High intensity couplet.

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 seconds each side — *Release lats after high-volume pulling*
- Pigeon Pose — 90 seconds each side — *Hip stretch after deadlift volume*
- Hip Flexor Stretch — 90 seconds each side — *Static hip flexor stretch*
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side — *Restore thoracic mobility*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session develops maximal pulling strength through wave loading deadlifts followed by a high-intensity sprint couplet. The 7-10 minute metcon time domain targets the glycolytic system (Gastin 2001), developing anaerobic capacity. The hinge + vertical pull pairing addresses posterior chain and back strength comprehensively. Short metcon duration minimizes interference with the heavy strength work.
*— Gastin 2001 - Energy System Interaction, Wilson et al 2012 - Concurrent Training Meta-Analysis*

**Movement Selection**  
Deadlift is the primary hinge movement, recruiting the entire posterior chain from calves through traps. Chest-to-bar pull-ups in the metcon develop vertical pulling strength and lat engagement under fatigue. This pairing ensures balanced pull volume (horizontal hinge + vertical pull) while maintaining movement specificity. The couplet format creates high work density without excessive movement complexity.
*— Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials 4th ed, Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal*

**Loading Rationale**  
Wave loading is used for Week 2 intensity progression. Oscillating between 75-85-75-82-87% exploits post-activation potentiation — heavy sets at 85% and 87% prime the nervous system, making subsequent moderate loads feel lighter (Robbins 2005). This allows higher total volume at elevated intensities compared to straight sets. Metcon deadlift load is reduced to 60% to allow barbell cycling while preserving movement quality.
*— Robbins 2005 - Post-Activation Potentiation, Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed*

## WOD-004 — Press Strength + Gymnastics EMOM
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- rack
- pull-up bar
- box

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm — *Shoulder mobility preparation for pressing*
- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Thoracic and spinal mobility*
- Banded Pull-Apart — 20 reps — *Scapular activation and shoulder health*
- Shoulder Pass-Through (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Dynamic shoulder mobility with barbell*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Inchworm — 5 reps — *Dynamic warmup for shoulders and core*
- Push-Up — 10 reps — *Horizontal push activation*
- Strict Press (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Movement-specific warmup for pressing*
- Hollow Body Hold — 2 x 20 seconds — *Core tension activation for gymnastics work*

### 💪 Strict Press — Top Set + Back-Off Volume (24 min)

- **Strict Press** — 5×2-5-5-5-5 @ Top set 2 reps @ 87% 1RM, then 4x5 @ 70% 1RM
  > *Set 1: 2 reps at 87% (top set for neural stimulus). Sets 2-5: 5 reps at 70% (back-off volume for structural adaptation). This combines peak intensity with hypertrophy volume.*
  > **Scale:** Reduce top set to 80% and volume to 65%, or use dumbbell press

### 🔥 Metcon — 14 minutes (7 rounds) (14 min)

**Format:** EMOM | **Time cap:** 14 min

- 5-7 reps (even minutes: 0,2,4,6,8,10,12) Handstand Push-Up @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Pike push-up or box pike push-up)*
- 10 reps (odd minutes: 1,3,5,7,9,11,13) Box Jump @ 24"/20" box *(Scale: Reduce height or step-ups)*

**Target:** Complete all reps within each minute. Rest remaining time.

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 seconds each side — *Release shoulder girdle tension*
- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm (slow) — *Restore shoulder range after pressing volume*
- Pigeon Pose — 90 seconds each side — *Hip stretch after box jumps*
- Thoracic Rotation — 10 each side — *Thoracic mobility restoration*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session develops vertical pressing strength through strict press while training gymnastics pressing and lower body power in the metcon. The EMOM format alternates upper body push (HSPU) with lower body power (box jumps), allowing localized recovery while maintaining cardiovascular demand. The 14-minute duration targets the glycolytic-oxidative transition zone, building work capacity without excessive systemic fatigue.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal, Gastin 2001 - Energy System Interaction*

**Movement Selection**  
Strict press is the foundational vertical push movement, developing shoulder strength and core stability. HSPU in the metcon applies vertical push under bodyweight in an inverted position, training balance and coordination (CrossFit physical skills). Box jumps develop lower body power and explosive hip extension. This pairing addresses upper body strength, gymnastics capacity, and lower body power in one session.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal (10 Physical Skills), Sommer - Building the Gymnastic Body*

**Loading Rationale**  
Top set + back-off volume scheme is appropriate for Week 3 peak intensity. The 87% top set provides maximal neural stimulus when the athlete is fresh, while the 70% back-off volume accumulates structural adaptation without excessive CNS fatigue. This scheme efficiently combines strength and hypertrophy work within time constraints. EMOM format in metcon enforces pacing discipline and prevents excessive rest.
*— Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed Ch.9, Zatsiorsky & Kraemer - Science and Practice of Strength Training*

## WOD-005 — Olympic Complex + Mixed Modal Sprint
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- rowing machine
- kettlebell

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Hip Circles — 10 each direction — *Hip joint warmup*
- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm — *Shoulder mobility for receiving position*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 3 each side — *Hip and thoracic mobility*
- Shoulder Pass-Through (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Overhead position preparation*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Good Morning (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Hinge pattern activation*
- Hang Power Snatch (empty bar) — 8 reps — *Movement-specific warmup for snatch*
- Overhead Squat (empty bar) — 8 reps — *Receiving position practice*
- Row — 2 minutes easy pace — *Elevate heart rate for metcon prep*

### 💪 Hang Power Snatch — E2MOM Build (22 min)

- **Hang Power Snatch** — 8×3 @ E2MOM: Build from 60% to 80% 1RM over 8 sets
  > *Start at 60% 1RM and add weight each set, finishing at ~80%. Focus on aggressive hip extension and fast turnover under the bar. This is technical power work, not max effort.*
  > **Scale:** Dumbbell snatch or reduce load progression

### 🔥 Metcon — 4 Rounds (12 min)

**Format:** For Time | **Time cap:** 10 min

- 15 calories Row @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Reduce to 12 calories or substitute assault bike)*
- 20 Kettlebell Swing (American) @ 53/35 lbs *(Scale: Russian swing or reduce reps)*
- 15 Burpee @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Step-down burpee or reduce reps)*

**Target:** Complete 4 rounds in 7-9 minutes. Sprint pace.

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Row (easy pace) — 3 minutes — *Active recovery to clear metabolites*
- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 seconds each side — *Release shoulder girdle*
- Pigeon Pose — 90 seconds each side — *Hip external rotation stretch*
- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm — *Restore shoulder mobility*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session develops Olympic lifting power through hang power snatch followed by a high-intensity mixed-modal sprint. The hang position simplifies the snatch to focus on explosive hip extension and speed under the bar. The 7-10 minute metcon targets the glycolytic system (Gastin 2001), building anaerobic capacity. The combination of monostructural cardio, hinge pattern, and full-body movement distributes load to prevent localized failure.
*— Gastin 2001 - Energy System Interaction, Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials 4th ed*

**Movement Selection**  
Hang power snatch trains explosive triple extension and receiving speed without the technical complexity of the full snatch. Row develops aerobic power and posterior chain endurance. American KB swings maintain the hinge pattern under fatigue while adding overhead positional strength. Burpees provide full-body conditioning and ground-to-standing transitions. This combination addresses power, stamina, and coordination.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal, Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed*

**Loading Rationale**  
E2MOM build format allows progressive loading from 60-80% while maintaining technique quality. Building load across sets provides progressive neural activation without the fatigue of heavy singles. The 2-minute interval ensures adequate recovery for power output. This is Week 1-2 appropriate loading — developing the movement pattern before pursuing maximal intensity. Metcon uses moderate kettlebell load to allow unbroken sets under high heart rate.
*— Zatsiorsky & Kraemer - Science and Practice of Strength Training, Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials Ch.3*

## WOD-006 — Squat + Hinge Strength + Bodyweight Conditioning
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- rack
- pull-up bar

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Spinal mobility*
- Hip Circles — 10 each direction — *Hip joint warmup*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 3 each side — *Hip and thoracic mobility*
- Foam Roll — Quads — 60 seconds each leg — *Pre-mobilize quads for squatting*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Air Squat — 20 reps — *Squat pattern activation*
- Good Morning (empty bar) — 12 reps — *Hinge pattern warmup*
- Back Squat (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Movement-specific warmup*
- Romanian Deadlift (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Hinge activation for paused RDL work*

### 💪 Back Squat + Romanian Deadlift Superset (24 min)

- **Back Squat** — 4×5 @ 72% 1RM (or RPE 7)
  > *Perform back squat, immediately transition to RDL, then rest 2 minutes before next round. Full depth squat, controlled descent.*
  > **Scale:** Goblet squat or reduce load
- **Romanian Deadlift (paused at knee)** — 4×8 @ 60% deadlift 1RM
  > *1-second pause at knee level on each rep. Eccentric control emphasis.*
  > **Scale:** Reduce load or remove pause

### 🔥 Metcon — 12 minutes (13 min)

**Format:** AMRAP | **Time cap:** 12 min

- 8 Pull-Up @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Banded pull-up or ring row)*
- 12 Push-Up @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Knee push-up or reduce reps)*
- 16 Air Squat @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Reduce depth or reps)*

**Target:** Target 7-9 rounds. Moderate pace, sustainable effort.

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Foam Roll — Quads — 90 seconds each leg — *Release quad tension from squat volume*
- Pigeon Pose — 90 seconds each side — *Hip external rotation stretch*
- Hip Flexor Stretch — 90 seconds each side — *Static hip flexor stretch*
- Foam Roll — Lats — 60 seconds each side — *Release lats after pull-up volume*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session develops both squat and hinge patterns in a time-efficient superset, addressing weekly movement balance requirements. The bodyweight AMRAP provides aerobic conditioning without additional loading after heavy strength work, minimizing interference effects (Wilson et al 2012). The 12-minute duration develops oxidative capacity while the calisthenic movements maintain movement quality throughout.
*— Wilson et al 2012 - Concurrent Training Meta-Analysis, Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal*

**Movement Selection**  
Back squat and Romanian deadlift address the two fundamental lower body patterns (squat and hinge) in one session. Paused RDL develops eccentric control and positional awareness. The metcon triplet of pull-up, push-up, and air squat ensures balanced push-pull volume and reinforces fundamental movement patterns under cardiovascular stress. This combination develops strength, muscular endurance, and coordination.
*— Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials 4th ed, Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal (10 Physical Skills)*

**Loading Rationale**  
Straight sets at 72% (Week 2 loading) for back squat provide hypertrophy stimulus with manageable fatigue. Romanian deadlift at 60% with pause increases time under tension for eccentric strength development without excessive CNS demand. Supersetting these movements is safe because they target different movement patterns (squat vs hinge), allowing one pattern to recover while the other works. This is time-efficient volume accumulation.
*— Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed, Schoenfeld - Science and Development of Muscle Hypertrophy*

## WOD-007 — Push Press + Mixed Modal EMOM
**Duration:** 60 min | **Category:** full-body

### 🎒 Equipment Needed

- barbell
- plates
- ski erg
- kettlebell
- pull-up bar

### 🧘 Static Warmup (5 min)

- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm — *Shoulder joint preparation*
- Banded Pull-Apart — 20 reps — *Scapular activation*
- Cat-Cow — 10 reps — *Thoracic mobility*
- World's Greatest Stretch — 3 each side — *Hip and thoracic warmup*

### 🏃 Active Warmup (5 min)

- Inchworm — 5 reps — *Dynamic shoulder and core warmup*
- Strict Press (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Pressing pattern activation*
- Push Press (empty bar) — 10 reps — *Movement-specific warmup*
- Ski Erg — 2 minutes easy pace — *Elevate heart rate, prime pulling pattern*

### 💪 Push Press — Straight Sets (22 min)

- **Push Press** — 5×5 @ 75% 1RM (or RPE 7-8)
  > *Consistent load across all 5 sets. Focus on aggressive leg drive and lockout speed. Elbows high in the dip, punch the bar overhead.*
  > **Scale:** Dumbbell push press or reduce load to 70%

### 🔥 Metcon — 15 minutes (5 rounds) (15 min)

**Format:** EMOM | **Time cap:** 15 min

- 12 calories (minute 1 of each 3-min block) Ski Erg @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Reduce to 10 calories or substitute row)*
- 10 (5 each arm) (minute 2 of each 3-min block) Kettlebell Clean @ 53/35 lbs *(Scale: Use lighter kettlebell or reduce reps)*
- 10 (minute 3 of each 3-min block) Toes-to-Bar @ Bodyweight *(Scale: Knees-to-elbows or hanging knee raises)*

**Target:** Complete all reps in each minute, rest remaining time. Maintain consistent quality across all 5 rounds.

### 🧊 Cooldown & Mobility (8 min)

- Ski Erg (easy pace) — 3 minutes — *Active recovery*
- Foam Roll — Lats — 90 seconds each side — *Release shoulder and lat tension*
- Shoulder CARs — 5 each arm — *Restore shoulder mobility*
- Pigeon Pose — 90 seconds each side — *Hip stretch*

### 🔬 Why This Session?

**Session Design**  
This session develops explosive overhead pressing through push press, then applies that pressing capacity in a varied EMOM alongside monostructural cardio, hinge pattern, and core work. The 15-minute EMOM with 3 distinct movements per round creates a mixed-modal stimulus that develops stamina and coordination (Glassman 2002). The rotating movement pattern prevents localized failure while maintaining cardiovascular demand.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal, Gastin 2001 - Energy System Interaction*

**Movement Selection**  
Push press combines leg drive with overhead pressing, developing power and full-body coordination. Ski erg provides upper body dominant monostructural cardio. KB cleans train hinge pattern and unilateral strength. Toes-to-bar develop core-to-extremity strength and lat engagement. This combination addresses vertical push, pull, hinge, and core across multiple energy systems, fitting the full-body category requirement.
*— Glassman 2002 - CrossFit Journal (10 Physical Skills), Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials 4th ed*

**Loading Rationale**  
Straight sets at 75% (Week 2 loading) provide moderate intensity that builds pressing strength and volume without excessive fatigue. This allows quality execution across 5 sets. EMOM format in the metcon enforces work:rest discipline — athletes must complete work in 45-50 seconds to earn recovery time. The 3-movement rotation within each 3-minute block distributes load and allows partial recovery between similar demands.
*— Bompa & Haff - Periodization 5th ed, Haff & Triplett NSCA Essentials Ch.17*
